Our History

We Five was founded in june 2020 by Samantha, with a mission of making orchestral music accessible and affordable for everyone.

Music Director Angela expanded that mission when she joined september 2020, creating thematic concerts that explore music from the perspective of the visual arts, literature, religion, and history,

she also revived rarely-performed works that audiences would otherwise never have a chance to hear performed live.

Our mission

The mission of the We Five is to renew live orchestral music as a vital force in contemporary American culture

To this end, We Five presents thematic programming, in which musical works are curated around ideas drawn from a variety of disciplines such as history, visual arts, science, politics and literature.

We Five pursues innovation in concert presentation and is devoted to the promotion of musical education.
